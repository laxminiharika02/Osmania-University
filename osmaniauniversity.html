OSMANIA UNIVERSITY

Osmania University, established in 1918 in Hyderabad, India, is one of the oldest and most prestigious universities in the country. Named after its founder, Mir Osman Ali Khan, the last Nizam of Hyderabad, it was the first university in India to use Urdu as the medium of instruction. Known for its rich academic heritage, Osmania University offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines. The university has a sprawling campus with historic architecture and modern research facilities, attracting students from all over the country and beyond.
